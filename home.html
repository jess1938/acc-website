MIT Individualized Tutoring for English and Citizenship

MIT Individualized Tutoring for English and Citizenship (ITEC) is a cost-free naturalization interview tutoring service where MIT students work 1-on-1 with residents in the Greater Boston Area seeking US citizenship. Our tutors provide personalized help to students with their specific goals for practicing English and the naturalization interview.

Our tutors are MIT students, who are all fluent in English and have additional language capabilities for Spanish, Mandarin, Vietnamese, and more. All tutors are trained in citizenship tutoring and eager to serve the community!

Schedule

  • Classes for the Fall 2021 term will run for nine weeks, from 10/4/21 to 12/17/21
  • Each student will be paired with one tutor for the whole term. Pairs meet for one hour/week.
